Welcome Chasers & Hunters!

We'd like to welcome you to Scooter's site for chasing storms and hunting ghosts! Chasing things that most people run from is exciting and rewarding! It brings an adrenaline rush better than the strongest energy drinks and makes for an exciting time! Whether it be virtually, at out camps, or on a live chase or hunt, we hope that you will find ways to join us on our chasing and hunting journeys!

Please do not do any storm chasing or ghost hunting without proper equipment and safety precautions!

Our Awesome Programs!

Chaser Cruise

Cruise around to some of the most notorius storm sites known to man!
See Mount Peer, the largest active volcano near the Hawaiian coast!
Visit the Coast of Oust where Hurricane Kelly caused major damage! Some of it still under repair!

All these amazing sites and so much more on this awesome Chaser Cruise!
